Alfredo Sauce

Description

This is a basic guide on how to make Alfredo sauce. This is a good beginner recipe as it's simple and easy to make. And once you try a homemade recipe you'll never want to go back to pre-bought stuff.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
